ABOUT US

ABOUT THE
FINAL THIRD

At The Final Third, we believe every talented player deserves a chance to prove themselves on the right stage. Founded by an experienced professional who understands the challenges of breaking into professional football, our agency bridges the gap between aspiring athletes and life-changing opportunities. We connect players with trials at reputable clubs, providing guidance, exposure, and the support needed to make the most of every opportunity. Whether you’re looking for your first break or a second chance, The Final Third is here to help you take that final step toward your dream.

Founder and CEO

“Born and raised in San Diego, I grew up chasing the dream of playing professional football. I know firsthand how difficult it can be to get the right opportunities, no matter how much talent or dedication you have. That’s why I founded The Final Third—to give players from all over the world a real chance to prove themselves. I’ve been in their shoes, and I understand what it takes to succeed. Our mission is to connect hardworking athletes with professional trials and provide the guidance they need to take their game to the next level. My dream is to help others achieve theirs."


— Cole Walker, Founder & CEO
